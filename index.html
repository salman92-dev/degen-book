The Vision

DegenBook Bot

VISION

Offer a wholistic ecosystem

Our vision behind DegenBook is to offer a wholistic ecosystem, by degens for degens, that allows you to explore insights on degens, devs, influencers, and more. Users can use our unique peer-to-peer voting and submission system for the latest trustworthy data, set up profiles, create crews and submit projects, effectively turning this into a large-scale social trust network for crypto investors. DegenBook is essentially a social proof based, one-stop place, for degens.

Aim

Build a huge ecosystem

What we aim to achieve through this is building a huge ecosystem where all degens can hang together, have fun, find crews to be apart of in order to make it, and know who to trust and who not.

SERVICES

Advertise themselves

We wish for DegenBook to allow users to advertize themselves as raiders, callers, influencers, designers and all sorts of services that are being sold over Telegram. Reputable developers can rank up and submit their genuine projects. Hard-working degens can climb the ladders in order to find teams or devs to work with.
